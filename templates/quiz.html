Quiz Strategy Tips

  • Take a quiz after studying to reinforce what you've learned.
  • Don't be discouraged by wrong answers; they help identify areas for improvement.

  • Set goals for your quiz scores and work to improve them over time.
  • Use the quiz results to guide your future study sessions.
