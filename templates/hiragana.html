Introduction to Hiragana

Hiragana is one of the Japanese writing systems. It's a phonetic script used for Japanese words, grammatical particles, and word endings. Each character represents a syllable.

Tips for Learning Hiragana

  • Practice writing each character multiple times.
  • Learn in small groups of 5-10 characters at a time.
  • Associate each character with a word that starts with that sound.
  • Use the audio feature to get familiar with the pronunciation.
  • Practice reading simple texts written in hiragana.
