Study with Flashcards

Flashcards are an effective way to build your Japanese skills through active recall. Click on a card to flip it and reveal the answer.

Effective Flashcard Usage

  • Try to recall the answer before flipping the card.
  • Study in short, frequent sessions rather than long marathons.
  • Focus more on the cards you find difficult.
  • Say the answers out loud to reinforce learning.
  • After successfully recalling a card several times, move on to more challenging content.
