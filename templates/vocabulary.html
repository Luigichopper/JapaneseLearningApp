Tips for Learning Vocabulary

  • Learn words in context rather than in isolation.
  • Group related words together to make associations.
  • Use spaced repetition to review words at optimal intervals.
  • Create sentences with new words to reinforce your learning.
  • Use the flashcard and quiz tools to test your retention.
